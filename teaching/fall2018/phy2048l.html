Grading

Grading is based on two components: 50% of your grade will be determined by participation (i.e. attendence) and 50% of your grade will be determined by your lab reports, with the lowest lab dropped. This means that you can miss one lab and not have it count against your final grade. Each lab report is included at the end of each experiment; all you have to do is fill in your data and your calculations. As long as you follow instructions and ask questions when you're stuck, I'll make sure you're doing the labs correctly.
